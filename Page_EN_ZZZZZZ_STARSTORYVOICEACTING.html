Hello boys and girls!

From the very first version of Star Story, it was my desire to add voice acting, just like the Fairy Tale has, however, circumstances were not kind on me, so I could not do it. Now that the new (and hopefully final) version of the game is about to appear, perhaps I should try to get some voice actors.

Since this is an amateur project without any kind of budget involved, I won't set high demands, but a minimum requirement is that you have mastered the pronuciation of the English language properly. Please read the role desciptions well, as a few roles may have a few more requirements.

Now the playable characters will likely have the most lines, not only scenario, but perhaps also some lines in battle. Characters with (B) behind their name will also appear in the game as a boss enemy. And here's the overview

  1. Wendicka Lovejoy
    1. Human female
    2. Main protagonist
    3. Tomboy
    4. Rude, yet golden hearted
    5. Very intelligent.
    6. Wendicka does understand some alien languages, and this may come back in a few lines.
    2. English
